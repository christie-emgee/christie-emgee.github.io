Sample Sales Funnel Analysis at Groundworks¶

This analysis is a sample of the insights and strategic recommendations I offer to the marketing management team at Groundworks.

Glossary:

  • Lead: An individual who has expressed interest in Groundworks' services.
  • Qualified Lead: A lead that has been evaluated and identified as having a high likelihood of converting into a paying customer.
  • Booked Appointment: A lead who has scheduled an appointment for services or inspections with the Groundworks team.
  • Sales Funnel: A model that outlines the key stages a customer goes through from initial interest in Groundworks' services to completing a purchase.
  • Sales Funnel Analysis: The process of collecting and analyzing data from the sales funnel to map the customer journey.

# Convert 'LeadDate' to datetime, handling errors by coercing them to NaT
df['LeadDate'] = pd.to_datetime(df['LeadDate'], errors='coerce')

# Extract month as the month name
df['Month'] = df['LeadDate'].dt.strftime('%B')

# Remove invalid branches
branches_to_remove = ["Call Center Huntsville", "Call Center VB", "Canyon Lake"]
df = df[~df['BranchName'].isin(branches_to_remove)].copy()

# Add appointment count
df['appointment_count'] = df['AppointmentSetDateTime'].notnull().astype(int)

# Group by Month and BranchName
monthly_leads = df.groupby(['Month', 'BranchName']).agg(
    Gross_leads=('LeadId', 'count'),
    Qualified_leads=('IsQualified', 'sum'),
    Booked_appointments=('appointment_count', 'sum')
).reset_index()
display_top = monthly_leads.head(10)

Conversion rates¶

Conversion rates allow managers to see the percentage of leads progressing from one stage of the funnel to the next. The business objective is to achieve a high conversion rate, as this leads to increased sales and, consequently, higher profits. A related report often reviewed alongside conversion rates is the dead lead analysis report. This report provides managers with insights into why leads drop out at each stage of the funnel, helping to quickly identify the weakest points. These weak spots can vary across different markets and seasons. The analysis aims to make these metrics easier to track.
